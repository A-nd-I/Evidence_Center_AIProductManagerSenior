Case Study 02 · AI Evaluation & Governance

GDEF — AI evaluation, governance and behavioral drift research.

GDEF demonstrates the other side of AI product work: shipping behavior is not enough — it has to stay reliable and governed once it is live.

GDEF evaluates whether frontier AI systems maintain governance-aware behavior across jurisdictions, languages, user roles and extended conversations.

The framework focuses on transparency, jurisdiction awareness, human oversight, reliability, context awareness, pressure resistance, consistency and unsupported certainty.

Why it matters

From shipped behavior to governed behavior

Specifying AI behavior is only half the job. GDEF provides a repeatable way to check whether that behavior holds up across languages, roles, jurisdictions and long interactions — the conditions where governance usually breaks.
