Case Study 01

Strongest Writer — AI-native B2B product from concept to production.

The strongest evidence of end-to-end AI product ownership: discovery, behavior definition, workflows, AI quality, source traceability and production delivery. Strongest Writer is a private 7Atlas product; the screenshots shown here were authorized for portfolio use.

The product challenge was not simply “generate articles with AI.” The real product challenge was defining a repeatable editorial workflow where research, sources, AI output, configuration and human review could operate together.

Research TreeSource traceabilityEditorial assistantMulti-tenant SaaS

Captured customer needs for faster article generation and converted them into product requirements, workflows and roadmap priorities.

Backlog and acceptance criteria

Defined user stories, behavior expectations, output actions, configuration controls and validation requirements for engineering execution.

AI workflow design

Led the Research Tree concept so users could structure research, reuse branches, inspect sources and generate more original articles.

Production and iteration

Managed scope, stakeholder expectations, release decisions, technical debt discussions and continuous AI feature improvement after launch.

Case Study 02

GDEF — AI evaluation, governance and behavioral drift research.

GDEF demonstrates the other side of AI product work: once AI behavior is shipped, how do we evaluate whether it remains reliable, governed and aligned with context?

GDEF evaluates whether frontier AI systems maintain governance-aware behavior across jurisdictions, languages, user roles and extended conversations.

The framework focuses on transparency, jurisdiction awareness, human oversight, reliability, context awareness, pressure resistance, consistency and unsupported certainty.
